What is Bet-Games?

This is the name of our project consisting in creating a bot on the famous Discord chat software. Indeed, focused on video games, this software offers us the opportunity to develop multiple services in this field.

Following this, we came up with the idea of taking advantage of the boom in online betting and the leader in competitive games, League of Legends. A lot of players take to the battlefields to compete and prove that they deserve their place on the podium, so why not spice things up?

What if we told you that you could make money from your favorite professional gambling teams? Bet, Analyze and Play using our Discord bot that takes care of your bets! Come and see how our bot works so you can master it to perfection.

OPERATION

Step 1 : Add our bot to your favorite discord server by clicking on the button below, login to your account and select the server of your choice.

Step 2 : Once your bot is operational, use your text room to communicate with the bot. The prefix to use is "#" followed by the name of the desired command.

#COMMANDS

Names Description
#info To have a reminder of the list of available commands directly on the text room
#connection To connect to a game
#games To know the available games
#teams To know the available teams
#odds To know the odd
#bet To make a bet
#mybets To know the current bets
#leaderboard To know the leaderboard
#wallet To know your wallet :
Servers list "BR1", "EUN1", "EUW1", "LA1", "LA2", "NA1", "OCE", "OC1", "RU1", "TR1", "JP1", "KR", "PBE"

#connection

[player's_nickname_that_you're_watching (writen in one word )] [region]

Step 3 : You are now ready to bet! To connect to the game of the player of your choice, perform the #connection command without forgetting the region (see the available list).

To help you find a game, you can use the website Porofessor.gg .

Step 4 : You can from these two commands, know the ID of the game played (several games are available), the team name (blue or red) and the composition.

#bet
